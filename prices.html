Editing offer

This offer is focused on editing already existing pictures. Depending on the size of the files shared, or more precisely the number of pictures, and the extent of the work required, the price might vary. With this offer we will have several calls to understand your needs for this editing and we will find a way to create exactly what you have in mind. For this kind of offer the physical presence is not required, but a stable internet connection to discuss is.

Photoshooting offer

With this offer we will work together in different sessions. The first will be a brainstorming session, where we will try to understand completely each other and have the chance to get in touch with our affinity. I think that, especially in these kind of works, complicity and chemistry are extremely important, to take out from both artists the best side of themselves. Therefore, if it isn't a match, nothing will be charged for the session. Moving to the second and third sessions, they will be strictly photoshooting sessions, aimed to find different locations to emphatise the model or company uniqueness. The first session will be in a chosen closed space, while the second in a chosen open space.

Price range: 200-700 euros.
